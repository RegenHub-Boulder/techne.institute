# Member FAQ — Documents and K-1s

---

## Where do I find my K-1?

Go to the Documents section of the portal at /intranet/documents/. Your K-1 documents appear there once uploaded by the steward. They are listed with the tax year and a Download button.

K-1s are typically uploaded by March 15 following the tax year.

---

## I see my K-1 listed but the Download button says "Contact steward"

The download link requires a storage configuration step on our backend. If you see "Contact steward" where a download button should be, email **steward@techne.studio** and we'll send you the file directly. This is a temporary state while we complete the setup.

---

## What is a Schedule K-1 (Form 1065)?

Schedule K-1 reports your share of the cooperative's income, deductions, and credits for the tax year. You need it to complete your federal tax return.

Because RegenHub, LCA is taxed as a partnership under Subchapter K, each member receives a K-1 rather than a W-2 or 1099. The K-1 shows:
- Your share of ordinary income or loss
- Deductions and credits passed through to you
- Your ending capital account balance (tax basis)

---

## When are K-1s issued?

By March 15 following the tax year. If you haven't received yours by March 20, contact **steward@techne.studio**.

---

## Do I need to do anything with my K-1?

Yes — give it to your tax preparer or enter the values on your federal return. If you don't have a tax advisor, note that K-1 treatment under Subchapter K can be complex (qualified vs. non-qualified allocations, basis tracking, self-employment tax treatment). Consulting a CPA familiar with partnership/cooperative taxation is recommended.

---

## I have a question about my K-1 figures

Start with your steward. Email **steward@techne.studio** with:
- Your name
- The tax year in question
- The specific line item you have a question about

For complex questions (e.g., basis discrepancies, self-employment income treatment), you may also want to consult your tax advisor.

---

## What other documents are in the vault?

In addition to K-1s, the vault may contain:
- **Bylaws** — the cooperative's governing document
- **Formation** — Articles of Organization and state filing
- **Other** — any additional documents the steward has uploaded for member access

---

## I'm a Class 4 investor. Where is my portfolio view?

The Venture Basket is at /intranet/ventures/ — it shows your committed capital, current value, MOIC, and individual positions. It is only accessible to Class 4 members. If you have a Class 4 interest and cannot access this page, contact a steward.
