About this research
Framing
Todd Youngblood is exploring a technical advisor relationship with Parachute, focusing on enterprise / organizational product research. The LCA's operational needs — governance, patronage accounting, multi-agent coordination — serve as a concrete specification for the enterprise platform.
Design partner thesis
A cooperative concentrates every enterprise requirement into a small, high-trust, high-accountability context. What works for a 10-member cooperative works for a 500-person law firm. What breaks at the cooperative scale breaks before it becomes visible at enterprise scale.
