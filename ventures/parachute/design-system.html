Callouts

Design note: The accent green is the primary signal color. Use it for affirmative states, completed items, and primary interactive elements.

Sky: Secondary signal. Use for in-progress states, informational callouts, and secondary patterns.

Amber: Medium priority or cautionary context. Not an error state — a considered deferral.

Voice & Framing

These documents sit at the intersection of two voices: Parachute Computer's open, airy technical precision and Techne Studio's grounded, situated perspective. The aesthetic carries both.

Parachute's epigraph — "The mind is like a parachute — it doesn't work if it's not open" — sets the register: accessible, curious, unhurried. Our documents inherit this. Dense technical content is welcome; so is silence between ideas.

Lead with the thing before you explain it. State the requirement, then the rationale. Show the component before you document it. Parachute.computer does this well — we follow.
