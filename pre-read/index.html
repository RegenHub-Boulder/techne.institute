Before We Sit Down

March 11, 2026 · Lunch · Boulder

Thank you for making time for lunch. Here's who we are and what we'd like to talk about.

Who We Are

RegenHub, LCA is a Colorado Limited Cooperative Association — eight founding organizers building a venture studio and project ecosystem from 1515 Walnut. We've been on the second floor since May 2024.

Techne is what RegenHub is building next: technology education, venture incubation, and a rotating calendar of programs and convergences. Same cooperative, new capacity — opening from the third floor.

Three Conversations

We have three things on our mind for lunch, each building on the last:

The lease transfer. Moving our second-floor lease from West Software to RegenHub, LCA — aligning the contract with the cooperative entity now doing the work.
The third floor. Leasing the third floor to open Techne — coworking, programs, and venture incubation. We have over $130,000 in committed capital from investors who are also participants in the work.
The longer view. A conversation about the future of 1515 Walnut — where we hope this partnership goes over the next two to three years.

We're not asking for decisions on all three tomorrow. We're sharing the full picture so everyone at the table sees the same horizon.

We come prepared and we come in good faith.
We look forward to sitting down together.
