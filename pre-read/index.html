Before We Sit Down

March 10, 2026 · 1:00 PM · Boulder

Thank you for making time for lunch. Here's a little context on who we are and what we'd like to talk about.

RegenHub

RegenHub, LCA is a Colorado Limited Cooperative Association — the legal and organizational foundation for everything described here. Eight founding organizers, committed investors, and legal counsel in place. RegenHub is a public benefit entity with the stated purpose of cultivating scenius — the creative intelligence that emerges when skilled people share sustained proximity. RegenHub has operated from 1515 Walnut since May 2024 and continues as the cooperative's home base on the second floor.

Techne

Techne is what RegenHub is building next — a venture studio and project ecosystem that opens to the public from the third floor. Techne supports technology education through LeanVibe.Build, incubates early-stage ventures, and hosts a rotating calendar of programs and convergences. It is a DBA of RegenHub: same cooperative, new capacity.

What the Third Floor Opens Up

The third floor is where Techne comes to life — a multi-modal space designed to evolve with what Boulder needs. Three capacities define it:

Coworking. An expanded daily workspace for members and affiliates. Desk memberships, meeting rooms, and shared infrastructure generate steady monthly revenue and bring a working community into the building.
Programs. A rotating seasonal calendar. First up: LeanVibe.Build — helping people rediscover their love of the craft of code. The space is designed for what comes next: technology cohorts, cultural convergences like Sundance Boulder, civic workshops, and programs at the intersection of bioregioning and civics.
Ventures. The cooperative incubates early-stage technology ventures. Each contributes a minimum 1% annually back to the cooperative, creating a shared basket that compounds over time.

We have committed capital, an operating model, and we're already in the building. We are seeking a lease on the third floor that reflects a long-term partnership — where the growth of what happens inside benefits everyone connected to it.

Over lunch, we'd like to walk through our plan for the third floor and explore the terms of a lease that works for everyone at the table. We come prepared and we come in good faith.

We look forward to sitting down together.
