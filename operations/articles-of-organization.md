## Section 5 — Purposes

The purposes for which this limited cooperative association is formed are:

> **To cultivate scenius** — the collective intelligence that emerges from sustained proximity of culturally aligned people — by operating as a cooperative venture studio that composes patterns of coordination, care, and value creation with intention and skill, enriching the economic and social habitat of its members, ventures, and community.

The cooperative pursues this purpose by:

- Providing physical space, legal infrastructure, capital access, and planning support for autonomous ventures
- Building and stewarding shared coordination infrastructure (tools, patterns, knowledge) as a commons
- Allocating surplus through patronage based on multi-capital contribution — labor, expertise, capital, and community — rather than ownership alone
- Distributing governance democratically: one member, one vote, regardless of capital contributed
- Operating with concern for the cooperative's ecological, economic, and social habitat beyond immediate membership

This public benefit purpose is binding on the Board of Directors. Directors must balance financial performance against it when making decisions, and the cooperative must publish an annual benefit report assessing its performance against this purpose.
